Frequently Asked Question

Who Can Apply?

Humanitarian Affairs invites all outstanding young leaders, between the ages of 20 to 30 years, who can demonstrate the following qualities to apply for selection to participate in The World Top 100 Young Leaders Forum in the University of Oxford.

  • A full-time student from one of the Top 100 universities in the world rankings.
  • Applicants must have a Grade Point Average (GPA) score of 3.5 and above.
  • Applicants must hold a volunteer or work-related position at their university or in their community or workplace.
  • Applicants must have experience in public speaking.
